Art is a Therapy

Out of numerous forms of therapy, art is one of them.

Whenever you're feeling low or need to refresh yourselves (why use therapy only when depressed), just pick up a paintbrush to paint the canvas of your dreams, a pen or a pencil to sketch your favourite movie character or why not try some doodling.
It will definitely make you happy and calm your mind.You will get those refreshing cool and calm vibes.

And hey, don't worry if your art doesn't look perfect, imperfections are damn beautiful. Just like a beam of light passing through a broken glass.
